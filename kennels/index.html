Safe Haven for Abandoned Dogs and Cats

Harrelson Kennels provides shelter and homes for dogs and cats that have been abandoned and discarded. We strive to help every animal that comes through our doors. As a result, all of our dogs and cats are referred to as our pets.

    Accommodations provided for each pet include

  • vaccinating,
  • medicating,
  • spaying and neutering,
  • determining specific meal plans for each pet depending on their nutritional needs,
  • microchipping,
  • allowing pets to have a chance to be adopted out through our fostering program,
  • having a vet on call 24 hours a day, and
  • providing end-of-life care when pets reach the final hours or days of their lives.
