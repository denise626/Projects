Pushing Purple Daisies

Pushing Purple Daisies is an unusual name, and we know this. The germination of the brand stems from a sweet little cat named Daisy. Daisy was a tiny little thing that never let her size determine her social position amongst the other cats, often fending off cats two and three times her size.

Unfortunately she passed away a few years ago but I never forgot her tenacity and I wanted a way to always remember her in the future, hence our name.

We strive to punch above our weight class and deliver goods in a timely manner. We strive to leave the same impression on you so that when you think Unique, Vintage, or Quality, you think Pushing Purple Daisies!!!

100% of all proceeds of our sales will go to benefit the animal facilities and rescues listed below.
